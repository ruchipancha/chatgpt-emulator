Best Practices for Interacting with ChatGPT

1. Be specific: "Suggest three graduation gift ideas under $50 for a friend who studied computer science."

2. Use open-ended questions: "What are some unique and thoughtful graduation gifts that would leave a lasting impression?"

3. Brainstorm ideas: "List 10 potential graduation gifts for a friend who loves art, and I'll narrow down my favorites."

4. Request iterations: "I liked the idea of a custom-made artwork. Could you provide more options or variations of this gift idea?"
